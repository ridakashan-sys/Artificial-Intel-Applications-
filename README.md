# Artificial-Intel-Applications
Course Overview
This repository contains materials, notes, and insights from the Artificial Intelligence Applications course. The course focuses on understanding how AI is currently being used across industries, what challenges remain unsolved, and the broader impact—both positive and negative—of AI systems on society.
The course objective was
- Explore real-world applications of AI across multiple sectors
- Analyze problems currently being solved and those still open
- Understand ethical implications, bias, and risks in AI systems
- Examine future possibilities, including large-scale and multi-agent AI systems
We focused on three domains (Solved Used Cases of AI, Ongoing Challenges, Issues Created) of the following industries
- Healthcare: diagnostics, predictive care, drug discovery
- Finance: trading, fraud detection, credit scoring
- Media: recommendations, AI-generated content
- Smart Cities: traffic, energy, safety
- Blockchain: secure & transparent AI systems
- Retail: personalization, inventory, demand forecasting
- AGI: human-level intelligence challenges
